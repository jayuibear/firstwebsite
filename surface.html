Parametric Surfaces

Well, there shouldn't be anything too new. We've dealt with surfaces many times before, as functions of x and y. Here's the problem though. Lots of surfaces and objects in real life aren't that forgiving. If you have a sheet of metal or some sort of screw, chances are it's going to fold over itself, or you need to find properties about vectors against the surface in case that it does. So, the question is, how do we define these sorts of surfaces?

You could try defining them implicitly.
